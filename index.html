Welcome to Checkers.

The goal is to eliminate all of the opponent's checker pieces.

There will be two players, one black and one red.

The player who gets their checker piece across the board will become "king".

The "king" is able to manuever forwards and backwards.

Once all of the opponent's checker pieces have been eliminated, you've have won the game.
