Welcome to Pong! In this version of Pong, there is a point system and there are different gaming modes.

In this game, you will be hitting the ping pong ball back and forth before one of the players misses their shot.

There will be two players playing hitting the ball and every time you hit the ball to the other player, you recieve a point.

Once one of the players reaches 50 points or if one of the players loses, restart the game and play again.

If you want to change the gaming mode, choose the desired mode and enjoy.
